Our Manifesto.

Habitat is a home for creative souls, united by a single goal - to contribute to the development of our city, country, and the planet.
We approach design with flexibility. There is nothing stable nowadays. Thus we adjust our design approach to every client, every project, and every task.
We are not just service providers, we are partners. We partner with startups and product companies who believe that design can solve any user problem. We help them to serve their users and get better.
We work with the products we believe in. Today we contribute to the ideas, that will take the lead tomorrow.
We strive to work with people who are interested in what they are doing and are ready to experiment. Design is all about trying something new. We value those who are not afraid of changes.
